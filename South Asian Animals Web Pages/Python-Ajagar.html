Ajagar(Indian Python)

The largest snake in the Indian subcontinent.

The Ajagar is a species of python found in the Indian subcontinent. It is the largest snake in the Indian subcontinent and is considered to be quite dangerous. The Ajagar is a non-venomous snake and kills its prey by constriction. It is a very powerful snake and can kill large animals like deer and wild boar. The Ajagar is a very good swimmer and can be found near water bodies. Ajagar is a very good climber and can climb trees with ease. The Ajagar is a very good hunter and can hunt at any time of the day or night. Ajagar is a very good ambush predator.
